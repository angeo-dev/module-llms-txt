# Angeo_LlmsTxt
Magento 2 LLMs TXT Generator

---

## Overview

Magento 2 LLMs TXT Generator allows store owners to easily generate an llms.txt file, improving how AI systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude understand and interact with your store content.

This module ensures your store’s information is more accessible and interpretable by large language models, enhancing AI-driven search results, recommendations, and conversational interactions.

Key Features

Customizable Content Selection: Admins can choose which entities—such as products, categories, and CMS pages—are included in the llms.txt file.

Company Branding & Descriptions: Define your company name, add descriptions, and provide additional context to better guide AI systems.

Automated Scheduling: Set how often the llms.txt file is generated to keep content up-to-date without manual intervention.

Status Tracking: Easily monitor the file generation status and ensure your data is always current.

Benefits

Improves AI understanding of your Magento 2 store.

Helps AI platforms generate more accurate, relevant responses about your products and offerings.

Reduces manual workload by automating file generation and updates.
